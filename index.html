About us

TRANSITIONAGE is an NGO that focuses on mentoring Youth in their transition age(Teenagers).

We do this through sharing testimonies of people that have made mistakes before and have overcome. These testimonies help to have these youth learn from those mistakes, relate with these testimonies and know that they are not alone.
