About Us

TRANSITIONAGE is an NGO that focuses on mentoring Youth in their transition age(Teenagers)

We do this through sharing testimonies of people that have made mistakes before and have overcome. These testimonies help to have these youth learn from those mistakes, relate with these testimonies and
know that they are not alone.

We know that people in their Transition Age are going through a phase of self Discovery and so alot is going on in their lives. They are faced with alot of behaviors that grow into habits and end up becoming addictions which once not managed can destroy them completely and so TRANSITIONAGE is here not to judge them, but help them on how to avoid getting into anything that will affect them and destroy their future.

TRANSITIONAGE, does guidance and counselling, conferences, seminars, youth camps, mentorship programs, one on one sessions, all tailored to help the youth in their transition age go through this phase
